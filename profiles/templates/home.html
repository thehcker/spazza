About Seamless Corporate Accounts

The Seamless Team

Seamless is an online food ordering service that makes it easy to feed the office. We simplify the process of ordering meals and paying for those purchases. As a result, we save our clients up to 30 percent on food-related costs, while making everyone happier and more productive. Perfect for individual employee meals, group orders, lunch catering and catering for corporate meetings and events, Seamless currently feeds thousands of hungry offices across more than 40 US cities and London.

We make hungry offices happy.

We're committed to making hungry offices happy. We take that mission seriously and that's good news for you.

From our innovative ordering and billing solution that will save you time, money and hassle to our world-class client support that will exceed your service expectations from Day 1, the Seamless experience is unmatched. We call it Food Happiness and we want to deliver it to you.
